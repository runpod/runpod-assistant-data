# Runpod Refund and Credit Policy

## Credits Are Non-Refundable

Per Runpod's Terms of Service, credits added to your account are **non-refundable**. This applies to:

- Credits purchased directly
- Credits from promotional offers
- Unused balance remaining in your account

## What To Do If You Have Issues

If you experienced a problem that resulted in unexpected charges:

1. **Document the issue**: Note the pod ID, timestamps, and what went wrong
2. **Check for pod issues**: Review your pod logs and usage history
3. **Contact support**: Email help@runpod.io with details of the issue

While credits are non-refundable as a general policy, Runpod support may review cases involving:
- Platform outages or bugs
- Billing errors
- Pods that failed to start but were charged

## Preventing Unexpected Charges

### Set Spending Limits

Configure spending limits in your account settings to prevent runaway costs.

### Use Auto-Stop

Enable auto-stop on pods to automatically terminate after a period of inactivity.

### Monitor Usage

Regularly check your usage dashboard to track spending.

## Account Closure

If you close your Runpod account, any remaining credit balance will not be refunded or transferred.

## Getting Help

For billing questions or to discuss specific charges:
- Email: help@runpod.io
- Include your account email and relevant pod/job IDs
