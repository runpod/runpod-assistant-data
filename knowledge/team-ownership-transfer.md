# Transferring Runpod Team Ownership

## Current Process

Transferring ownership of a Runpod team is not available as a self-service feature. To transfer team ownership, you must contact Runpod support.

## How to Request a Transfer

1. **Email support**: Send a request to help@runpod.io
2. **Include required information**:
   - Current owner's email address
   - New owner's email address
   - Team name or ID
   - Reason for transfer (optional but helpful)
3. **Verification**: Both the current and new owner may need to confirm the transfer

## What Gets Transferred

When team ownership is transferred:
- Administrative control of the team
- Billing responsibility
- Access to team settings and member management

## What You Can Do Self-Service

While ownership transfer requires support, team owners can:

### Add Team Members

1. Go to Team Settings
2. Click "Invite Member"
3. Enter email and select role

### Change Member Roles

Team members can have different permission levels:
- **Admin**: Full access to team settings
- **Member**: Can create and manage resources
- **Viewer**: Read-only access

### Remove Team Members

1. Go to Team Settings > Members
2. Click the remove button next to the member

## Contact Support

For ownership transfers or complex team management needs:
- Email: help@runpod.io
- Include your account email and team details
